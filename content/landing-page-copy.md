## HOW IT WORKS

### Section Label
How It Works

### Section Headline
**From question to clarity in three steps**

---

### Step 1
**Describe your challenge**
Type a question, a decision, or a situation you're wrestling with. No templates, no forms — just tell Panely what you need help thinking through.

*Example: "Should I raise a seed round now or stay bootstrapped for another 6 months?"*

---

### Step 2
**Your panel gets to work**
Panely assembles the right mix of specialists and runs an advisory session. Each advisor brings their expertise — and their candid opinions. They debate, challenge each other, and pressure-test every angle.

*Specialists include: strategy, trading, engineering, legal, research, creative, QA, and more.*

---

### Step 3
**Walk away with a Board Brief**
Panely synthesizes the session into a clean, shareable Board Brief: key recommendations, action items, dissenting views, and next steps. Export it as PDF or share the link.

*Done in under 15 minutes.*

## FEATURES / VALUE PROPS

### Section Label
Built for how you actually decide

### Section Headline
**Everything a great advisory board gives you — without the scheduling hell**

---

### Feature 1
**Specialists who disagree**
Real advisory value comes from friction, not consensus. Your panel includes advisors who challenge each other's assumptions — so you hear the strongest version of every perspective, not just what you want to hear.

---

### Feature 2
**Pre-built panels for every situation**
Choose from curated panel templates — Growth Strategy, Technical Architecture, Investment Decision, Legal Review, and more — or build your own from scratch. Your panel, your problem.

---

### Feature 3
**Shareable Board Brief output**
Every session ends with a Board Brief: a clean executive summary with recommendations, action items, and dissenting views. One link. Share it with your team, your investors, your co-founder.

---

### Feature 4
**Deep dives on demand**
Any advisor can go deeper on the spot. Ask Counsel to spell out the legal risk. Ask Atlas to model the downside scenario. Panely keeps the thread and brings everyone back together.

---

### Feature 5
**Full session history**
Every session is saved. Search across past sessions, compare how your thinking has evolved, and pick up where you left off. Your advisory history is yours.

---

### Feature 6
**Action items that don't disappear**
Panely extracts concrete action items from every session and tracks them for you. No more decisions that evaporate after the meeting ends.
